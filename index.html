What is GetIt

GetIt is a marketplace that connects people who need help with nearby helpers. Post jobs like moving, errands, cleaning, or small tasks — and helpers can accept them instantly.

Features

Everything you need,
nothing you don't

01
Post jobs in seconds
Describe what you need, set a time and budget, and your listing is live — instantly visible to helpers nearby.
02
Find nearby helpers
GetIt matches you with trusted local helpers in your area so help arrives fast, without the hassle.
03
Real-time chat
Coordinate directly with your helper. Ask questions, share details, and confirm everything before they arrive.
04
Secure payments
Payments are handled securely through the platform. Pay confidently knowing your money is protected.
